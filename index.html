About Me

Having an interest in the arts and the sciences has been a bit of a blessing and a curse, starting from the day I read my first dinosaur book; I've acquired a lot of fantastic knowledge from both worlds, but deciding how to marry those two halves of my personality has been a bit of a challenge.

As I became an adult I realized the magic behind getting me hooked relied not only on the information itself, but also how it was presented: through imagery and storytelling. I've decided to pursue a career in web development so I may employ these ideas to improve science and art communication to wide audiences.
