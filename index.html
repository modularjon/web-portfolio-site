About Me

Ever since I stepped into my ‘dinosaur phase’ as a kid, I’ve had an addiction to the intersection of art and science. My childhood stacks of books and magazines were filled with evocative visual languages and storytelling, creating new worlds for me to explore. There core of visual art is creativity, but the images I fell in love with were also built on information, derived from the findings of scientific processes and the facts of the natural world.

Truth and beauty: trying to marry those two halves of my interests has taken me down many roads of learning. I’ve spent time studying graphic design, fine art, biology and medical illustration, but am shifting gears after seeing how powerful web media has become. I want to be immersed in the opportunity to build web applications that combine rich, interactive, engaging visual elements with information of real utility to the user.
